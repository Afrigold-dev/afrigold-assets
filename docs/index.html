About

AfriGold (AFGD) is a fixed-supply, community-driven token on Solana. A modest 1% transfer fee (with a hard cap per transfer) helps sustain development and operations.

Liquidity plan: initial seeding on Raydium, followed by periodic top-ups as community traction and volume grow. We’ll post brief transparency updates after each change.
